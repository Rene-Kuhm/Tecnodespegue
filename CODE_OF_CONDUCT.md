# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for impact.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior harms the project or community.
